Our Mission

Founded in 2015, Cowboy Infidel represents the hard working community of Central Oregon and every other one horse town across the United States. We are the "Cowboys" - farmers, ranchers, ropers, bull riders, saddle bronc riders, bareback riders, barrel racers, hunters, and off roaders. We are gun toting, flag waving, God fearing Patriots. We are the "Infidels" - a label given to us by the radical islamists that have sworn to destroy our freedoms and our way of life. We are proud to wear the title. Proud to stand up for the freedoms we often take for granted. In this ultra politically correct world we are proud to label ourselves a COWBOY INFIDEL!
